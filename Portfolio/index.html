About

Yino Liu is a designer transitioning from architecture into computational and interactive design. Her background includes architectural education, digital modeling, visualization, fabrication, and design research, with experience working across studio, office, and academic contexts. She is interested in how spatial ideas can be tested through drawings, interfaces, simulations, installation systems, and code-based tools. Her education in architecture shaped a careful approach to proportion, material, structure, and representation, while recent projects explore data, interaction, and visual systems as ways to make complex environments more legible.
